Game of PIG

Rules of the Game:

  • There are two players.
  • The player whose turn it is rolls the dice.
  • The total of the roll is added to the current player's score, unless either die comes up as a "one".
  • If a "one" is rolled, the player's turn is over, and it becomes the other player’s turn.
  • After each roll, the current player can either roll again (if no "one" was rolled) or pass to the other player.
  • The first player to get 30 points or higher wins.

Oh, and if you roll two "ones" (snake eyes), your current score gets zeroed out. So don’t do that!
