T% CORN DOG & BOBA!

T% is a bobashop that closes later in Davis, so it's definitely a place to consider if you're hungry at night! LUCKY CHARM

This is a must-have cereal or snack to always have in your house. The cereal itsself is not too sweet, but paired with the marshmellows just make it a perfect balance. Just pour some milk with it in a bowl and a delicious and fast meal is made. Trust me, a bowl or two makes you full.

This midnight snack idea's highlight is my mom's signature sauce!
