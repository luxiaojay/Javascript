Blog Post 2 - Your Article HTML

April 26th, 2023

The article by Vlado Pavlik that talks about semantic HTML and how to use it right. Basically, semantic HTML is super important for keeping things organized, making things accessible, and getting good SEO. I followed the tips in the article for my own piece and used different tags to divide it up into sections, with unique ids for each section that I could mess around with in CSS. The structure tags helped me keep everything in order, and I used those semantic text tags to make sure everything was in the right order of importance. I agree with Vlado about not relying on HTML to style text, even if the semantic tags do some of the work for you. You should use CSS to style

The most challenging aspect of visual design are choosing a color scheme and the various fonts that work well together, finding the right balance between text and images. Regardless, it’s also challenging since it’s important to also keep the user experience in mind and ensure that the design enhances the content rather than detracts from it.
